Short Bio

I am a PhD student working at the Department of Electrical Engineering at Linköping University (LiU) in Sweden. I am supervised by Kailai Li and Fredrik Gustafsson. My research interests lie in state estimation and uncertainty quantification. My previous and ongoing work has given me extremely great enthusiasm about research. I'm excited to get to know and develop cutting-edge techniques in related areas.
